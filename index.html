    About This Study

    Motivation

    The goal of this visualization was to explore how heart rate patterns vary across different meditation techniques, and whether factors like gender identity and session type (individual vs. group) influence these trends...

    Visualization Design & Encodings

    We use a line chart to show heart rate fluctuations over time for each individual, a choice driven by the effectiveness of position...

    Data Transformations

    Data is filtered dynamically based on selected meditation techniques and gender, using input controls...

    Interaction Design

    The interface includes technique & gender filters, toggle between individual/average view, and interactive legend...

    Design Rationale

    The visual encodings, interaction model, and color palette were chosen for clarity and accessibility...

    Development Process

    We used D3.js for rendering and interaction, focusing on performance and modularity...
